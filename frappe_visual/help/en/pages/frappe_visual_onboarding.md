2. Auto-Enhancers (Form, List, Workspace)
3. Visual Hub & App Maps
4. Graph Engine & Layout Algorithms
5. Data Visualization (9 chart types)
6. Scene Engine & Immersive Dashboards
7. Navigation & Command Palette (⌘K)
8. Layout Containers (Masonry, Bento, GridStack)
9. Micro-Animations (GSAP)
10. Whiteboard & Workflow Designer
11. Data Storytelling
12. Theme Builder
13. Icon System (5,000+ Tabler Icons)
14. Export & Sharing
15. Settings & Configuration
16. Roles & Permissions (CAPS)

Opens in a `frappe.visual.floatingWindow()` on the opposite side of the sidebar.
