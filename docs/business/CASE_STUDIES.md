# Frappe Visual — Case Studies

## Case Study 1: ERP Implementation Consulting

### Challenge
A consulting firm managing 15+ ERPNext implementations struggled with onboarding new team members. Understanding each client's customized data model took 2-3 weeks.

### Solution
Installed Frappe Visual on each client site. New consultants could visually explore the entire app structure, understand doctype relationships, and identify customizations in minutes.

### Results
- **80% reduction** in onboarding time (from 3 weeks to 3 days)
- **40% fewer** data model-related bugs
- **Client satisfaction** increased — visual demos during meetings

## Case Study 2: ISV Product Development

### Challenge
A SaaS company building on Frappe needed to document their complex data model for investors and partners. Static diagrams were always outdated.

### Solution
Embedded Frappe Visual's AppMap directly in their documentation portal. The visualization updates automatically as the schema evolves.

### Results
- **Living documentation** — always current
- **Faster code reviews** — reviewers can see relationship impact
- **Investor confidence** — interactive demos in pitch meetings

## Case Study 3: Training & Education

### Challenge
A Frappe training institute needed interactive materials for teaching ERPNext's data architecture to 200+ students per semester.

### Solution
Used Frappe Visual's Storyboard component to create guided walkthroughs of ERPNext modules. Students explore the real data model interactively.

### Results
- **90% student engagement** (vs 60% with static slides)
- **2x faster** concept comprehension
- **Reusable** across semesters (auto-updates with ERPNext versions)
